About Me

I’m Ali, a self-taught video editor with a unique style built over years of passion and experience. I’ve been doing this for years, long before I started making money from it, and I still dedicate time to personal projects just for the love of editing. My style isn’t just about making things look good; it’s about telling a story, creating the right rhythm, and building tension with each frame ,just like in movies.

I am an artist first and foremost. I focus on the little details that make a big impact, spending time perfecting every part of a video to make sure everything looks just right. Whether it’s a minor adjustment or a major scene, I’m committed to ensuring that every moment is meticulously crafted.

With over five years of experience, I’ve worked on a wide range of projects for channels like Motivation Mentors (200K+ subscribers), Business Finance Coach (100K+ subscribers), and iQraaly (2M+ users). I’ve edited for genres including finance, news, short documentaries, technology, vlogs, cooking, horror stories, and more. Throughout, I’ve produced 154 videos, garnered 24.55M views, and earned 538.61K likes by focusing on high engagement, precise pacing, and captivating visuals.

I’m also well-versed in YouTube’s copyright system and algorithm, ensuring that everything I create is optimized for the platform. Plus, I have access to top-tier royalty-free stock footage, graphics, and music through Artgrid and Envato Elements, so I can always bring high-quality resources to your project.
